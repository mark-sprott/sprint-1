Techniques I tried this week

So I found this subject very interesting, with lots of useful information and tips to help you stay focused and on target.

In the end I decided to pick the 3 techniques that interested me most. The Power of Meditation, Mindfulness, and the Pomodoro Technique.

The art of Mindfulness thinking is the subject that really interested me, and the one that I will be doing a lot more work on. I think this way of thinking will be very beneficial for work and personal life. However that said the Pomodoro technique has been the best technique that I have found to stay focused, and the idea of just targeting one thing for 25 minutes then taking a five minute break, and reflecting on what you achieved has been fantastic for me.

I will continue to experiment with all these techniques in the future.

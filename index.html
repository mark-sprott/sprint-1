MY EDA EXPERIENCE SO FAR!!

So the EDA experience has been fantastic. There has been alot of support and everyone is super supportive during a full on brain overload of a first week.

I have really enjoyed the collective approach to leaning. The key for me this week has been to slow down, read through the content slowly move through each task.

Coming into the academy once a week has really given me more confidence, and has eased my thoughts about bootcamp dramatically. I would encourage everyone to do it if possiable.

The culture at EDA is really open and feels really safe. I think that engaging with this type of culture will really suit me as I think it will most people. This really is the learning environment of the furture!!

So at the end of the first week i'm not sure if my expectations of EDA have changed, but the expectations of myself have. I am feeling more confident and am expecting bigger things of myself.

Am I excited about participating in this kind of learning environment? The simple answer to that is Hell Yes!!
